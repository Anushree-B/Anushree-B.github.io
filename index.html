Published Work

NeurIPS 2025 — Weather4Cast
Computationally-efficient Deep Learning Models for Nowcasting of Precipitation
A ConvGRU-based deep learning architecture for precipitation nowcasting using multi-spectral satellite imagery. Ranked 2nd globally in the NeurIPS Weather4Cast 2025 benchmark challenge.

Abstract: ConvGRU encoder-decoder with cross-domain transfer learning achieving 2nd place globally, demonstrating strong generalization across European sensor configurations.
